Password Pete was short and sweet,

all phrases he could repeat,

for all his accounts,

the password was the same,

when he got hacked once,

it was all hacked just the same.

Henry Hacker had a blast,

spending Pete's money quick and fast,

Pete's Amazon account was on fire,

Henry buying whatever he desired,

a new laptop, some RAM, it's all for free,

Thanks Pete for buying this for me!

Pete's Credit Card balance was a sore sight,

thousands of dollars racked up in one night,

He quickly put his credit card on freeze,

and called the customer service line that eve,

and oh what an awful and boring night,

disputing transaction after transaction till the sun shone bright

Password Pete learned his lesson in the end,

Never would his fate be this again,

Changing all his passwords was a feat,

oh, what a treat...,

Now Pete can get some sleep,

Knowing all his passwords are unique.

-Mike Berg
